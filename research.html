Research Areas

Noise & Acoustics

Prediction & Mitigation

Bridging aeroacoustic models and community metrics (SEL, DNL, EPNL). Tools include FAA AEDT, NASA AIRNOISEUAM, Helios, PSU-WOPWOP, and custom pipelines.

Routing

Dynamic Airspace Allocation

Multi-objective optimization of AAM corridors with constraints for safety, equity, and noise exposure. A* / K-shortest path diversity and altitude layering.

DAA

Vision-Based Detect & Avoid

Training YOLOv9/v11 on AVOIDDS for intruder aircraft detection; analysis with precision-recall, IoU, and confidence distributions.
